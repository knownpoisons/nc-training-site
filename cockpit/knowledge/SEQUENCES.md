# SEQUENCES.md — touch templates

Seed content from the handoff (F3 + Engine Zero). Editable via Slack later
("update touch 2 template: ..."), which writes to the `cockpit_templates` table
and back to this file. `[BRACKETED]` markers are gaps the Draft Engine fills or
leaves for Jem to personalise — the system never fakes personal knowledge.

---

## Base sequence (cold/outbound — days 1, 4, 12, 21)

**TOUCH 1** (connection note, LinkedIn, ≤40 words)
[PERSONALISE — one line proving you looked: campaign, hire, announcement]. I train creative teams to work with AI properly — thought your team might be at that point. No pitch, just connecting.

**TOUCH 2** (value, day 4)
[PERSONALISE — reference their reply or their work]. One number from the work: [PROOF STAT]. If you want to know where your team actually stands, this takes two minutes and doesn't pitch you: [SCORECARD LINK]. It'll just tell you.

**TOUCH 3** (ask, day 12)
Direct question: teams like yours usually hit the same wall — [PLAYBOOK: most common pain from sales calls]. Worth 15 minutes to see if that's you? [TWO TIME OPTIONS].

**TOUCH 4** (breakup, day 21)
Closing the loop — if the timing's wrong, no problem at all. The door's open when the team's ready. [ONE LINE: what to watch for that signals it's time].
