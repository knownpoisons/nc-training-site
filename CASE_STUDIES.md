## Process

1. A new number reaches a public page **only** after a CONFIRMED row exists here.
2. Each row needs: the figure, the client, what it precisely measures, the
   source, and that the client has cleared it for marketing use.
3. Client quotes need written approval on file; log them under APPROVED QUOTE.
4. Press-sourced claims must link the article and match its wording, attribution
   and scope exactly.
5. Re-audit both properties whenever a case study is added.
